GIS Journey:

I am a first-year PhD student in Geography and Urban Studies. This Spring semester I enrolled into Fundamentals of Geographic Information System with the hopes of learning a new skill to apply into my future research. Currently, my research interests are on anti-displacement strategies in gentrifying neighborhoods and communities.
